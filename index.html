SANJALIKA Slides & Rides

Our Slides and Rides section in the SANJALIKA waterpark provides you with unlimited fun and enjoyment with Friends and family.

Summit Plummet

With a near-vertical freefall of 120-feet, the 12-story Summit Plummet at SANJALIKA waterpark offers one of America's most thrilling water slide experiences. Slide at top speeds before going through a dark tunnel, ending in a mega splash. Before hopping out to plummet again, check the digital display at the bottom of the ride to see your peak speed on the slide.

Rattlesnake Rapids

For a top-notch family rafting ride, look to Rattlesnake Rapids at SANJALIKA waterpark, just outside Salt Lake City. Hop aboard a raft and you’ll find twists, turns and soaking waterfalls. Be prepared: everyone in the raft will get wet before this ride comes to an end. The rapids are gentle enough for little ones, but pack enough of a punch to be enjoyed by teens and adult riders.

Buy Ticket

Maximum Velocity

Enjoy splashing with friends? Then Maximum Velocity, the side-by-side, dueling water coaster ride at SANJALIKA waterpark, is an absolute must. The water coaster takes you up, down and all around as you race friends at top speeds through more than 800 feet of tandem tubes. Only serious thrill-seekers need apply.

Buy Ticket

Mammoth

Grab a seat on a 6-person round boat before screaming down the Mammoth, the world’s longest water coaster. This popular ride at SANJALIKA waterpark, IN, takes riders down 7 stories, going forward, backward and all around before splashing down at the end of the ride. This is definitely a ride to brave as a family.

Buy Ticket

Quadzilla

If it’s not enough to race against just 1 slider, hop on a mat and slide head first down Quadzilla, a 4-lane racing slide, WI. Twist, turn and speed through tunnels before splashing down and discovering which rider was first to the finish.

Buy Ticket

Kookaburra Kreek

Laze the day away in a tube along the relaxing currents of Kookaburra Kreek, a lazy river that spans 1/4 mile at Raging Waves. Float along the river for its entirety or hop out at 1 of several entrances, which are located near a water slide, a wave pool and a water coaster.

Buy Ticket

Leap of Faith

If you’re looking for a water slide that drops 6 stories before plunging you into a shark-infested lagoon, take a ride on Leap of Faith at SANJALIKA waterpark, part of Atlantis resort in the Bahamas. After sliding out of a Mayan temple, keep your eyes open as you quickly zip through a clear tunnel surrounded by sharks before finishing up with a big splash.

Facilities

At the SANJALIKA waterpark we offer you with:

Swimming Aids

Life vests are available adjacent to the Changing Village ramp. Please speak with a member of staff to obtain one for use. Please return before heading back to the Changing Village, we would hate for you to set off the alarm if you’ve “forgotten” to hand it back!

First Aid Facilities

A fully equipped first aid room is situated on pool side with qualified and trained staff.

Bars

Waterfalls Cafe and Bar, situated in the Mall, sells a range of alcoholic beverages as does the WatersEdge Snack Bar which is located inside the Waterpark.

Free Wi-Fi

Within both the Mall and Waterpark areas we have Wi-Fi access which is free of charge for all of our guests.

Toilets

We have male, and female toilets located both on pool side and within the changing rooms. There are fully accessible disabled toilets located in the waterpark, mall area and changing rooms. We also have an award winning, Changing Place Facility within our Waterpark. You do not have to be a SANJALIKA Waterpark guest to use this facility. If you wish to use it, simply speak to a member of our Reception team and they will be happy to arrange this for you.

Car Parking

At the SANJALIKA waterpark we provide free and convinent parking spaces for easy and free entry to all our guests.

Lockers

The SANJALIKA waterpark is equipped with electronic lockers which are allocated with an electronic wristband. The wristbands can also be used for Splash Cash purchases at our Catering outlets. We have small (2 people max) medium (3 people max) and large (families) lockers available.
There are also some lockers located on the pool side for our guests with disabilities.

Seating Areas

For guests not wanting to enjoy our slides and attractions, we have a selection of terraces and seating areas throughout the Waterpark offering great views of many of our rides and attractions. Why not grab yourself a drink from one of our catering outlets and watch your friends and family enjoying our slides and attractions. Entry into the Waterpark is by admission ticket, prices of which can be found on the price page of this website.

Shops

Essential items such as swim nappies, goggles and a small range of others are available to purchase from Reception. However, please ensure you bring everything you need for your visit. Towels, swimwear and floatation devices and goggles are the key essentials for a visit to our Waterpark, please ensure you have these with you.
Treetops Trading Post – A small selection of items will be available at Treetops Trading Post, pick up a souvenir before you leave!
